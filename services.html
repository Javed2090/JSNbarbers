Services & Pricing

A focused menu built around clean fades, beard work and classic shaves. No hidden extras — finish and styling are included.

Prices are transparent and the timing is realistic. If you’re not sure what to book, DM on Instagram or send a quick WhatsApp message with a photo of your current hair.

Hair services

  • Signature Skin Fade – £25 (45 mins)
    High, mid or low fade with clipper and scissor work on top, texturised and finished with product.
  • Classic Taper & Scissor Cut – £24 (45 mins)
    Softer, low-maintenance cut for work or study, with shape kept clean around the neck and ears.
  • Restyle Cut – £30 (60 mins)
    For when you’re changing your look completely — more time for consultation, sectioning and styling.

Beard & shave

  • Beard Shape-Up – £15 (25 mins)
    Clipper and scissor work with razor on cheeks and neckline, finished with oil or balm.
  • Fade & Beard Package – £32 (55 mins)
    Full fade and beard refinement in one sitting for a complete, sharpened look.
  • Clean Shave Ritual – £22 (40 mins)
    Hot towel, prep, classic straight-razor shave and hydrating finish for a proper reset.

Extras & offers

  • Student Midweek Cut – £20
    Available Tue–Thu before 16:00 with a valid student ID.
  • Line-up / Neck tidy – £10
    Quick clean-up between full cuts to keep your shape sharp.
  • Skin fade refresh – £18
    For regulars returning within 2 weeks of a full skin fade.

How to book: send a WhatsApp message with your name, preferred day, time and the service you want. You’ll get a confirmation and any alternative times if your slot’s already taken.

Please arrive on time — if you’re running late, just drop a quick message. If the slot can be held, it will be; if not, we’ll reschedule you to the next available opening.
