Visit & Booking

Central Birmingham location with WhatsApp-first bookings. Easy to reach from tram, bus and city centre parking.

Booking process

  1. Send a WhatsApp message with your name.
  2. Include preferred day, time and the service you’d like.
  3. Optionally attach a photo of your current hair/beard.
  4. Wait for confirmation and any alternative time options.

If you need to move or cancel your appointment, try to give at least a few hours’ notice so the slot can be re-opened.
